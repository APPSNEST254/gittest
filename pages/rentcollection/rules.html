  5. . All the house rent should be paid to the last cent within the stipulated time. The deposit shall not be utilized to offset unpaid rents but will be refunded when the tenant gives one-month prior notice of vacation.
  6. In case of power connection or construction hitch the room in the room or premise, tenants are advised to inform the agency and not meddle with the problem.
  7. All rooms/premises rented/leased should be used for the agreed intended purpose i.e. If a room is for commercial purpose it should not be used for any other purpose and vice versa.
  8. In case of transfer from one tenant to a new tenant, the later and the former should come to the agency for agreement schedule.
  9. In case of furniture and other property in the room. The tenant shall maintain them in the right conditions and should promptly pay the forth coming charges emanating from damage or alterations safe for ordinary wear and tear.
  10. Avoid wastage of water and use power saving mechanisms. Do not make subsidiary writing/ connections on the wall.
  11. Do not drive nails into the wall and do not use glues on the walls or ceilings. While vacating leave the premises clean and maintained.
  12. For security purposes notify the agency while keeping your belonging inside the room while you are away. The agency shall not admit liability for theft out of breakage and or opening an authorized into the rental unit.
  13. Deposit shall NOT be used as rent. Upon vacation, the agency shall inspect the premise for any damage or alteration and thereafter refund of the deposit to the tenant within 14 working days from the date of vacation.
